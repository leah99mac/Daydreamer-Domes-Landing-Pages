LOCATION
Located just outside downtown South Haven Michigan, with only a 5 minute walk to Lake Michigan, DayDreamer Domes offers a refuge from the normal flow of life for oddities and artists. Only 2 hours from Chicago and 3 hours from Detroit.
LODGING
Stay in one of five geodesic gold domes, aka "Disco Domes". All domes have been assembled with high quality tent materials allowing for a glamorous camping experience. Each dome has a 14 foot ceiling, heat and air conditioning, full indoor bath, mini fridge and desk. No dome has the same interior design as each has their own aesthetic.
GROUNDS
Daydreamer domes has 150 acres to explore and have fun on. Our grounds include hiking trails, a heated pink pool and a clubhouse with restaurant seating for 70 people. It is BYOB around the outdoor lounge area and pool.
FOOD AND ART
At Daydreamer Domes, our goal is to merge art and life style. Througout the grounds, art created by artists local and abroad alike is scattered about. Find even more art inside our club house and at our restaurant. Enjoy a luxury meal created by our head chef, and experience the art of flavor while surrounded by visual art as well.
